## üõ† Prompt Writing Methods

PromptBani utilizes diverse methods to tailor the prompt to specific needs.

### A. Main Structured Prompt (General Purpose)
*This method converts any input (text, image, or video) into a structured and effective prompt.*
*   **Primary Subject:** Clear identification of main elements.
*   **Scene/Environment:** Detailed description of setting and context.
*   **Key Actions:** Determination of activities and interactions.
*   **Artistic Style:** Specification of visual or narrative style.
*   **Mood/Atmosphere:** Setting emotional tone and overall ambiance.
*   **Important Details:** Keywords and specific elements.

### B. Advanced Image Generation Prompt
*Specifically for generating high-quality images with AI models like DALL-E, Midjourney, and Stable Diffusion.*
*   **Core Description:** Detailed explanation of main subject.
*   **Lighting:** Type, direction, and quality of light.
*   **Color Palette:** Combination of dominant and complementary colors.
*   **Mood & Atmosphere:** Emotional space of the image.
*   **Artistic Style:** Various art styles (realism, impressionism, digital art, etc.).
*   **Technical Enhancements:** Technical details like resolution, aspect ratio.

### C. Advanced Video Generation Prompt
*For creating videos, animations, and motion content.*
*   **Core Description:** Main concept of the video.
*   **Camera & Framing:** Camera angle, shot type, and camera movements.
*   **Lighting:** Lighting conditions of the scene.
*   **Duration:** Video length.
*   **Artistic Style:** Visual style.
*   **Scene Sequence:** Order and transitions between scenes.

### E. Freestyle Prompt
The most flexible method with no fixed structure. The system writes prompts creatively and freely, suitable for innovative and unconventional ideas.

### F. JSON-Based Prompt
*Professional structure for developers and advanced users.*
*   **Structured JSON format:** Organized arrangement of all parameters.
*   **Precise settings:** Complete control over all prompt aspects.
*   **Processable:** Suitable for API and script usage.
*   **Automatic optimization:** System automatically optimizes the prompt.

### G. HTML-like Tagging System
*Organized and structured method similar to HTML coding.*
*   **Custom tags:** Use of meaningful tags.
*   **Clear hierarchy:** Hierarchical organization of elements.
*   **High readability:** Easy understanding of prompt structure.
*   **Extensible:** Ability to add custom tags.
